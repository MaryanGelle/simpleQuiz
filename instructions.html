Welcome to a fun quiz game

Simple ways to complete our general knowledge game!

  • A beginner friendly quiz game that lets you challenge your mind in under a minute.
  • You will be given 10 multiple-choice questions to answer.
  • Choose one answer from the four options provided for each question.
  • Only after selecting an answer can you press the "Next" button to move to the next question.
  • On the final page, you will see your total score out of ten.
  • The username you enter is temporary and only used while playing. It is not stored permanently.
  • Your high score is saved locally on this device. It will remain until your browser cache or local storage is cleared.
  • Press the "Play Again" button to try another round. There are no limits!
  • Have fun playing!
